About The MUN

Empowering Youth Through Diplomatic Discourse

IGNIS is more than just an event it is a movement of ideas; action,and transformation. As our flagship initiative, IGNIS brings together Model United Nations, skill-based workshops, and futuristic debates under one platform. It embodies our fiery,futuristic, and transformative identity, ensuring that participants walk away not only with skills but with purpose

IGNIS empowers youth to not just discuss change but to create it, blending diplomacy with innovation.
